Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who can use PiNmates?

Anyone above 18 years of age can use the application.

2. Do I have to take any subscription to use the application?

No. PiNmates is a free to download and use mobile application. There are 2 types of registrations in the application i.e. individual and business. Both the types of registration are free however, businesses who wish to list themselves on the app are required to take the subscription.

3. Who can register on PiNmates as an Individual user?

Anyone who wants to seek/share information regarding his neighbourhood or explore businesses/ISPs in his PIN code can enjoy the application as an individual user at free of cost.

4. Who can register on PiNmates as a business user?

Any local business or individual service provider who wish to expand his business at a nominal cost can create business account and list his business on app.

5. What is the procedure to list your business on PiNmates?

You can list your business on app by creating your business account and submitting the required details and documents. Your business listing becomes active on the app within 24 hours of submission of details.

6. What is the mode of payment for subscription to business listing on the application?

Mode of payment on application is online only. We do not accept cash payments.

7. Can I access forum of any PIN code in our country?

No. You can access the forum of a particular PIN code in which you are currently present.

8. Are there separate mobile applications for individual users and business users?

No. There is a common application for both the types of users. Business user can also register his individual profile and use it in the application simultaneously with business profile.

9. Our business has various branches and we wish to register all on PiNmates. Is there any other way to register all the branches on application other than registering one by one?

Yes. You can contact sales team on Pinmatesofficial@gmail.com for hassle free bulk listing and discount offers.

10. What is the use of PIN forum?

You can seek/share information via forum with other active users in your PIN code.
